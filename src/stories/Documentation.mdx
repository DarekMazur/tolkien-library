    <h1>Tolkien Library – Polish Editions &amp; Related Works</h1>
    <p>
      Welcome to the <strong>Tolkien Library</strong> project!
    </p>
    <p>
      This web application is dedicated to cataloguing and presenting comprehensive information about Polish editions of J.R.R. Tolkien’s works, as well as publications about the author and literature inspired by his legacy.
    </p>
  </header>

  <section>
    <h2>About the Project</h2>
    <p>
      <strong>Tolkien Library</strong> was created to serve as a central, easily accessible source of knowledge for readers, researchers, and collectors interested in the Polish publishing landscape of Tolkien’s works.
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li>All known Polish editions of Tolkien’s works (translators, publishers, publication dates, cover art)</li>
      <li>Publications about Tolkien (biographies, essays, critical studies)</li>
      <li>Books inspired by Tolkien’s legendarium or scholarship (including fan and academic publications)</li>
      <li>Search and filtering tools to easily find editions, authors, or topics</li>
    </ul>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h2>Key Features</h2>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Database of Polish Editions</strong> – detailed records of all editions, including translators, publishers, dates, and covers</li>
      <li><strong>Publications about Tolkien</strong> – descriptions of biographies, essays, and studies published in Poland</li>
      <li><strong>Related Publications</strong> – books inspired by Tolkien’s work or research</li>
      <li><strong>Search and Filtering</strong> – quickly find items of interest</li>
      <li><strong>Resource for Collectors and Fans</strong> – a reference point for expanding collections or deepening knowledge about Tolkien’s presence in Polish culture</li>
    </ul>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h2>Motivation</h2>
    <p>
      This project fills a gap in access to well-organized information about Polish editions of Tolkien’s works and related literature.<br/>
      Polish editions are notable for their diversity, unique translations, and rich publishing history-Tolkien Library documents and celebrates this heritage.
    </p>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h2>Technologies</h2>
    <ul>

      <li><strong>Linting and formatting:</strong> ESLint, Prettier, Husky, lint-staged</li>
      <li><strong>Testing:</strong> Vitest</li>
      <li><strong>Documentation:</strong> Markdown, Storybook, JSDoc</li>
    </ul>
  </section>
